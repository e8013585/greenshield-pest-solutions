About GreenShield

At GreenShield Pest Solutions, we protect your home and family using environmentally responsible methods that are safe for people, pets, and the planet.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, GreenShield Pest Solutions emerged from a simple idea: protecting homes shouldn't harm the environment. Our founder saw a need for truly eco-friendly pest control after struggling to find chemical-free options for her own family.


Today, our team continues to pioneer new methods that eliminate pests while preserving the health of your family and the planet.
